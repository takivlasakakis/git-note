### Relationships between **Classes**


#### Composition
* has_a
* one contains or owns the other
* the aquarium `has_a` fish

#### Inheritance
* is_a
* shared_parent
* a hula hoop `is_a` toy, and a Frisbee `is_a` toy

#### Shared Module
* can
* shared ability or behavior
* a superhero and a donkey `can` both kick
